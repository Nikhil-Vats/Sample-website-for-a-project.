THE BOSM 2K18

We love sports!

BITS Open Sports Meet (BOSM) is the annual sports festival of Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani campus. This sports meet, usually held around mid-September is a prominent showcase of sporting talents from around the country and abroad too. One of the largest student managed Sports fests of the country, BOSM is aimed to provide a platform to colleges from all over India to exhibit their true potential, promoting healthy competition.
